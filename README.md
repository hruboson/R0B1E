# R0B1E

`en`

Let's go on a journey with *R0B1E*! Play as a small robot working for its master, the Landlord. Take care of janitorial apartments and help tenants in a world that is too far gone. But R0B1E does not mind! Its job is to help after all.
This game was made during the 
48-hour long Game Jam Zlín 2026.

- Made by Karavana Team
